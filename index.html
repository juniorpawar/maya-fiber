About
Shree Maya
Fiber

Shree Maya Fiber is a leading name in the cotton industry, dedicated to delivering premium-quality cotton products with a strong focus on sustainability and ethical sourcing.

As a trusted partner for businesses worldwide, we specialize in a diverse range of cotton and textile solutions, including organic cotton Yarn, BCI-certified Cotton & yarn and Fabric , PSCP & REGENAGRI-certified products, GRS-certified Cotton-yarn, premium cotton bales.

Our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and responsible sourcing sets us apart, making Shree Maya Fiber Indore the preferred choice for high-quality cotton and textile solutions.

Our Product Range

At Shree Maya Fiber Indore, we specialize in a diverse range of cotton and textile products, sourced from the finest mills and ginners across India. Our offerings include

Organic Cotton /
Organic Yarn

We provide ethically sourced organic cotton and yarn, produced with environmentally conscious practices. Ideal for those seeking sustainability without compromising on quality.

BCI Cotton /
BCI Yarn

As a leading source of Better Cotton Initiative (BCI) certified cotton,yarn and Fabrics we ensure that our products meet global sustainability standards, fostering responsible farming and improving livelihoods.

PSCP &
REGENAGRI

We offer PSCP (Primark Sustainable Cotton Programme) and REGENAGRI certified products, promoting regenerative agriculture practices that nurture the soil and environment while enhancing product quality.

Combernoil
& GRS

Our combernoil and Global Recycled Standard (GRS) certified Cotton & yarns help promote a circular economy by reducing waste and ensuring traceability and responsible production.

Cotton
Bales

We source premium cotton bales, including varieties such as DCH32, MECH1, MCU5, and Shankar-6, known for their exceptional quality and superior spinning performance.

Cotton Made
In Africa

We proudly offer Cotton Made In Africa (CMIA) certified cotton and yarn, sourced sustainably from Africa to support livelihoods, create jobs, and drive economic growth.

Our
Presence
